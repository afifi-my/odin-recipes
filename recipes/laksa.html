Assam Laksa

amazingly complex flavor of sour, spicy, sweet, umami all in one.

Ingredients You Would Need: : 4 Servings

Coconut Milk Rice

  • 4 cups jasmine rice or any white rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk known as (santan)
  • 3 cups water add another 1/2 cup if cooking on the stove
  • 2 pandan leaves (screwpine leaves-knotted)
  • 1 stalk lemongrass (bruised)
  • Salt to taste

Fried Anchovies and Peanuts

  • 1 cup cooking oil (use for the sambal later)
  • 1/2 cup dried anchovies
  • 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts

Dried Anchovies Sambal

  • 1 cup cooking oil (use for the sambal later)
  • 1/2 cup dried anchovies
  • 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts

Garnish

  • 1 small cucumber, washed and sliced not too thinly.
  • 2 eggs, either hard or soft-boiled, cut in half
  • 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts
